INTRODUCTION
The following information is presented to showcase our skills and
experience in the field of architecture and construction. We are
passionate about achieving excellence in our commitment to our
clients by providing strong architectural design and high-quality
construction that stands the test of time.
Our drive to deliver innovative and forward-thinking solutions
motivates us to continuously expand our knowledge and improve the
planning and execution processes involved in modern construction
projects. We hope this selection of our work will help build new
professional relationships and open opportunities to collaborate in the
pursuit of new horizons.

=== PAGE 3 ===
CAPABILITIES
We have experience designing a wide range of civil and
architectural projects, especially residential, institutional, and
tourism developments. Each project begins with a detailed study
of the site, including functional requirements, desired spaces,
climate analysis, terrain characteristics, visual perspectives, cost
estimates, regulations, and permits.
PROJECT DESIGN
Our work is guided by a forward-thinking vision that promotes
creative, functional, and contemporary architecture. Each design
aims to combine innovation with practicality to deliver projects
that are both visually compelling and highly functional.
Our process also includes budgeting, planning, supervision, and
execution of construction projects. We coordinate different
professionals involved in architecture and construction to ensure
that every project delivers value to our clients and contributes
positively to the development of the city.
ARCHITECTURAL VISION
PROJECT MANAGEMENT
The conceptual stage defines the project’s form, structure,
materials, construction details, colors, textures, lighting, climate
systems, landscaping, environmental impact, and functional
requirements. These ideas are developed into architectural and
technical plans ready for construction.

=== PAGE 4 ===
SCHOOL
Design of an educational center for Comunidad de Esperanza, a
mission dedicated to working with children in the community. The
project is located in Villa Mella, Santo Domingo Norte, Dominican
Republic, and was designed by Architect Josué Mallén Lizardo.
Type : Institutional
The project features contemporary architecture that emphasizes
respect for nature. The main trees on the site were preserved, and
green walls, planters, and murals were incorporated to highlight
environmental awareness. Spaces were also designed to take
advantage of the natural breeze and climate of the location.
Concept & Inspiration

=== PAGE 5 ===
The complex includes a parking area with a guard booth, reception
area and administrative offices in a two-story building, two three-story
classroom buildings, a library and laboratories, a cafeteria, green
areas, sports courts, a multipurpose hall, playground areas, and other
facilities.

MULTIPURPOSE HALL
Design and construction of a multipurpose hall for the Church “Iglesia
de Convertidos a Cristo”, located in the La Julia area, Santo Domingo,
Dominican Republic. This hall was designed by Architect Josué Mallén
Lizardo.
Type : Institutional
It features a contemporary design with both natural and artificial
lighting, using the proper shapes of ceilings and walls to achieve
better acoustics. It also incorporates acoustic sheets and panels,
landscaping, automated colored lighting, TV screens, an audio
system, and video cameras for live internet streaming, as well as
an integrated audiovisual system connected to the main hall of
the temple. This multipurpose hall has a capacity of 65 people.
Concept & Inspiration

RESTAURANT
Design and construction of a restaurant located in the prestigious
Naco area, at the corner of Gustavo Mejía Ricart Avenue and Alberto
Larancuent Street. This space was conceptualized for two fast-food
brands that originated under the “food truck” concept.
Type : Commercial
This project unites the Rolling Steaks and Burger brand with the
Hummus Sabores del Desierto brand under one roof, offering guests
two distinct dining experiences in the same place. The layout keeps
the kitchens and service counters separate, while all shared areas—
dining room, terrace, restrooms, and parking—are designed for
everyone to use.
Concept & Inspiration

BUILDING
